Project 1: As We May Rethink

Using design to explore the imagined devices of “As We May Think,“ we will work toward a deeper understanding of the effort behind augmenting humanity with science and technology initially proposed by Vannevar Bush.

This project consist of three parts:

1. Draft a functional specification for one device mentioned in “As We May Think.“ 2. Develop user flow diagrams detailing at least one function of your chosen device. 3. Create an illustration of the device. 4. Develop a magazine advertisement for the device that fits in with the look and feel of magazines of the era in which “As We May Think“ was directly influential on those who set out to make Bush’s ideas reality, the mid to late 20th Century.
