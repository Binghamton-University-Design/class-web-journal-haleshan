My Data Portraits

Our normality of everyday life has been disrupted, and while our schedule continues to remain constant, enough has changed to make our time feel masked. “Waves of weirdness continue to erode the familiar rhythms of (our) world.” To this disruption, we have a response of how we will handle the wave. Will we ride, or dive under? These waves come in all different sizes, some big and intimidating, while others, tiny and calm. And, sometimes, the best solution to handle it is a little laughter. For this project, a quarantine quiz was sent out to friends and family. The data was collected and translated to eleven visual compositions individually representing the participants.
