Project 1, Part 1: Functional Specification

For this project, read through “As We May Think,“ and write down everything you can learn about one of the devices described by Bush. A good functional specification should include scope, solution overview, requirements specification, use cases, and non-functional requirements

Project 1, Part 2: User Flow

Using design to explore the imagined devices of “As We May Think,“ we will work toward a deeper understanding of the effort behind augmenting humanity with science and technology initially proposed by Vannevar Bush.

Develop user flow diagrams detailing at least one function of your chosen device.

Project 1, Part 3: Magazine Advertisement

Design a full color magazine ad for your chosen device. Situate the illustration you created for this device into the ad. Write a convincing marketing copy that explains what the device is, how it works, and why someone may chose to integrate it into their life. Work up an appropriate type hierarchy, and use solid, effective typography to help sell the idea.

Project 2: Transcoding Data

For this project we will develop a process that visually transcodes repsonses from a simple survey into a well designed, structured, visual composition. Essentially, a data visualization of responses from a questionnaire. This project consists of three parts:

1. Questionnaire Choose a concept for a short questionaire or survey.

2. Composition Design a visual composition consisting of variable parts representing all the possible permutations of survey answers. This design will be used as a template for your data visualization.

3. Data Visualization Build an interactive visualization applying structured data from your questionaire to your composition template to highlight different people’s responses.
